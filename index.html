Platform Engineering

Multi-Cluster GKE Platform Modernisation

Challenge: Managing Kubernetes clusters across multiple GCP projects required significant manual effort and made upgrades harder to standardise safely.

What I built: Implemented a more repeatable Terraform-based approach for provisioning and managing GKE clusters, improving configuration consistency and making upgrade planning more structured.

Impact: Reduced operational overhead, improved standardisation, and supported a more scalable cluster management model.

CI/CD & GitOps

GitOps CI/CD Delivery Platform

Challenge: Application delivery needed a more reliable and repeatable deployment model with better visibility, reduced drift, and safer rollout patterns.

What I built: Designed and supported CI/CD workflows using Jenkins, Helm, and ArgoCD to improve deployment consistency and enable a cleaner GitOps-style delivery approach for Kubernetes workloads.

Impact: Improved deployment reliability, reduced manual intervention, and made release workflows easier to operate and troubleshoot.

Automation & Operations

SlackOps for Incident Triage & Operational Visibility

Challenge: Engineers had to switch across multiple tools to investigate failures, fetch logs, and understand recent alerts, slowing down triage.

What I built: Developed Slack-based operational tooling to surface build failures, retrieve logs, and improve visibility into infrastructure issues directly from chat workflows.

Impact: Reduced friction during incident response and made common operational checks faster and more accessible for engineers.
