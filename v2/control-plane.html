Case / Multi-cluster GKE

Standardise the platform beneath the layers.

GKE provisioned across multiple projects can become hard to reason about when configuration, upgrades, and policy enforcement vary by environment. The operating model used Terraform as the source of truth, CI and OPA for validation, and a blue-green replacement model for safer upgrades.
